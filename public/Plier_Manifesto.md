# The Plier Manifesto: A New Culinary Taxonomy

Founder: The User  
Date: April 2026

## I. The Crisis of Classification

The hot dog is neither a stacked sandwich nor an unleavened taco fold. The Plier Method establishes a separate class based on structure and hand mechanics.

## II. The Plier Definition

A Plier requires three traits:

1. Leavened starch: Aerated bread, baked or steamed.
2. Unified hinge: One continuous piece acting as a cradle.
3. The pinch: Vertical squeeze stabilizes fillings during consumption.

## III. The Hierarchy

| Category | Representative | Starch Type | Action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Plier | Hot Dog | Leavened Bun | The Pinch |
| Taco | Street Taco | Unleavened Tortilla | The Fold |
| Sandwich | Club Sandwich | Sliced Loaf | The Stack |

## IV. Hall of Fame

- The King: American Hot Dog
- The Ancient Ancestor: Gyro
- The Coastal Variant: Lobster Roll
- The Eastern Variant: Gua Bao

## V. Sanctions and Exceptions

- Pizza slices may become temporary pliers in transit, but remain pizza.
- Calzones are closed pliers, or vaults.
- Tortilla-based items remain tacos.
